Lung Cancer Information

This page provides some key facts about lung cancer.

Symptoms

  • Cough that doesn't go away
  • Chest pain
  • Shortness of breath
  • If you experience any of these symptoms, please see a doctor.

Prevention

  • Not smoking tobacco is the best way to prevent lung cancer.
  • Avoiding secondhand smoke and air pollution can also help reduce your risk.

For more information about lung cancer, please visit a reputable health organization website.
